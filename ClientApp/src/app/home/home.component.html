Welcome to ProgressTracker

This application was designed to aid with tracking students' ability to work in a team. For such a purpose, there were a few mechanisms prepared:

  • Time reporting - used to track time spent on project by users. Helpful to determine if student spends too much or too little time on their work
  • Engagement points - used to specify the amount of work contributed into the project by teammates
  • Work descriptions - used by the student to specify their responsabilities and overall performance during work period. After submitting such a description, other teammembers might be abble to comment on it, if they find the description is incorrect
  • Surveys - used to determine what was the overall performance of the team. This is also the place to add any additional feedback about the project.

What do I do?

After visiting this site, you might ask yourself what are the next steps. Depending on your situation, you can (with Owner mode DISABLED):

  • Enter the 'Groups' tab and request to join a group, by inputting its code that was provided by the teacher
  • If you have already joined a group, you can list its projects by entering the 'Projects' tab and selecting the group you wish to list the projects for. Here you can also request a new project creation, if enabled by the teacher.
  • You can also perform various activities after entering the projects view. Click on various activies to expand them, then follow the instruction inside each of them.
